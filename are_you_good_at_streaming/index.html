...If I stand here, I might end up in the camera's field of view. I should leave at once

Oh? Nagumo's jacket is...

Eh? Whoa, you're right! Wait, does that mean it was like that during the stream, too!

Ugh, that's a bit embarrassing. I wonder if everyone was just being polite and didn't say anything...

Itsuki-senpai, thank you for telling me. You really saved me!

Hmm. Come to think of it, when I was practicing outside yesterday, I might have snagged my clothes on some kind of art object or statue

Good grief... Well, what's done is done

I was on my way to the bakery, so I shall take my leave now

Ossu! Thank you very much!
