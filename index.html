    6th Anniversary (2021)

    The 6th Anniversary is themed “FUSIONIC STARS!!.” This theme reflects a concept named "Fusion" and other concept named "Rock". "Fusion" stands for fact that every unit would be getting a "Fusion Song" with other unit, mixing their styles and bringing a brand-new concept. Rock stands for the music style of the 6th Anniversary song, "FUSIONIC STARS!!", which is a Rock themed song.
