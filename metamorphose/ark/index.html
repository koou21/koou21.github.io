Chapter 1

Time: One hour later
Location: At an airport near ES

...

Um, Anzu-san, what do you usually do on your days off?

“You already asked that”?

Then, maybe I’ll try asking a different question—

What made you want to become a Producer, Anzu-san?

“You already asked that too”? S-sorry, Anzu-san. I can’t even manage a proper interview—

P-please don’t fire me…! I’ve only got three lives left, so just count that one as a near miss, okay?!

How’s the documentary filming going, Tsuzura-kun?

The new project that’s about to be announced is a massive one I’ve carefully nurtured myself

I’m counting on you to capture every moment of this new era idol project ♪

Y-yeah, I’ll do my best—

The harder I try, the more I just spin my wheels and end up bothering Anzu-san though—

Uu… Just please don’t fire me, okay…?

Fufu. That will depend on your efforts, so I won't promise anything

So then, what kinds of questions have you been asking?

L-like… hobbies, or how she spends her days off, maybe…?

I just want to get along with Anzu-san first, so I’ve been asking about whatever comes to mind—

Hm. This isn’t a matchmaking meeting, it’s work, you know. Are you really alright with that?

Anyway. I’d like both Anzu-chan and Tsuzura-kun to witness the moment the Four Kings of the Sky are formed

It’s almost time to gather. The others should be arriving soon as well

Unfortunately, Itsuki-kun declined. But Rei-kun, who came early, will be followed by Rinne-kun and Mikejima-kun as well

Hm. I’m glad they’re coming soon. I was left out in the cold and at a bit of a loss here

By the way… Tsuzura-kun, was it? Why are you holding hands so affectionately with young Anzu over there?

It looks like you’ve suddenly gotten quite close. Care to explain what’s going on in detail?

Eh? Ah, s-sorry—

I’ve been clumsy since I was little, often getting separated from others, so I kind of developed this habit of grabbing the hand of whoever’s nearby

Besides, when someone holds my hand, their pulse helps me calm down…♪

With their pulse…?

Hm. Being friendly isn’t bad, but I’d say you’re a bit too close for comfort

Anzu, I’ve told you before, you shouldn't get too comfortable with someone who isn’t your lover

Oh? Compared to the old days at Yumenosaki, it’s nothing worth worrying about, is it?

Well, times and positions have changed. We’re respectable adults now, after all. Be careful not to let strange rumors spread

Y-yeah. Sorry, Anzu-san—

I’m glad you said you don’t mind, but I’ll try not to cling so much

It’s a habit, so I might not be able to fix it right away—

Tsuzura-kun, you sometimes hold my hand too, don’t you?

I take it as a sign of trust, so I don’t mind. But suddenly grabbing someone’s hand might startle them, so you need to be careful

R-really sorry…! Should I grovel on the floor too!?

How should I put it… you’re quite an eccentric one

Fufu. His restlessness is a flaw, but he’s still a promising talent

It would be a shame if his badge ran out of lives and he was dismissed under Thunderbolt’s policy. I’d hope for leniency

Wah—ah…! Anything but that!

If I got fired from Thunderbolt, I’d be completely lost with nowhere to go…!

I didn’t intend to make it such a big deal…

…Well then. Thanks to Tsuzura-kun’s eccentricities, the topic drifted off track

Since you came early, Rei-kun, I’ll share a bit with you

Though it’s more idle talk than anything. But I’d like you to be aware of the sense of crisis I’m about to mention

Are you familiar with the unit I produce?

MELLOW DEAR US. Wasn’t they your greatest masterpiece?

I sometimes go to Europe for family rituals, so I’ve heard of them

I wouldn’t call myself a fan, but they’re a unit that’s made a vivid impression overseas, right?

They haven’t really made it to Japan, right? It’s mostly overseas info—

The idols of this country evolved in a different form than overseas idols. No one thought the two would intersect in the present age

It could be said domestic idols were enough to satisfy demand here. But now, information about overseas idols flows in. That’s diversification

But surely they’re not coming to invade Japan. What does MELLOW DEAR US have to do with this sense of crisis of yours?

I mean that such an invasion could indeed happen

Oh…?

In this modern age where people get what they want as they wish, I can’t imagine them deliberately attempting cultural invasion—

Wasn’t the cooperation between Thunderbolt and ES meant to prevent exactly that?

Yes. That was my intention too. But the audience will make more realistic choices

The reasons may vary, but the existence of idols supported simply because “they’re from the same country” is unnatural in itself

Hm. Supporting not the idol themselves, but their nationality, isn’t the essence of it

Even for UNDEAD, with Adonis-kun among us, that’s not a mindset I can agree with

If there were an “Idol Japan Representative” or something, it might be a different story, though

Fufu. In that case, I’d prefer selection based on their base country, not just nationality

Anyway. You must grow in order to be chosen

My next plan—the Mega Sphere Project, is a completely different program from 4piece

MELLOW DEAR US was formed before the Mega Sphere Project

They didn’t cross the straits, so they never brought foreign idol culture into this island nation

But once the Mega Sphere Project is launched, that barrier will be removed

The Mega Sphere Project is that much of a powerful substance

At the same time, the Mega Sphere Project is also a gospel bringing fruit to the relationship between idols and producers

Japanese idols, too, will be able to more easily cross the sea and expand overseas

I wanted to bring such a wonderful plan into this country as quickly as possible

Otherwise, we cannot stand against MELLOW DEAR US. Without it, we’d be trampled helplessly by yet-unseen talents from overseas

That’s why I had no choice but to rush the plan into action

Before the idol industry of this country is destroyed
