You’re the one who left the birdcage open. If you dislike me perching on other trees so much, perhaps you should have joined the Audition yourself?

...Wait, why’s it getting so tense!?

Hey Yuzuru~! Do something about this atmosphere!

Even if you say that to me...

Eichi-sama, Hibiki-sama. I don’t know what has transpired between you two, but please don’t make young master feel uncomfortable
