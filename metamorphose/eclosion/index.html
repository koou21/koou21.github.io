Translation Notes

  1. ↑ During Tempest, fine members are working a lot and don’t have time to spend together, specially Eichi. So, as a solution, they create something called “Eichi Day”, a day off they would pass together.
  2. ↑ Here, madara says "ナイス・アルネブ・サンダー氏" (Nice Arneb Thunder-shi), in this case "氏" (shi) is rendered as “Mr.”, because it is not considered an honorific in Japanese. Unlike titles such as "さん" (san) or "様"(sama), which directly express respect or familiarity, "氏" functions as a neutral, formal marker used mainly in writing to identify a person by surname.
  3. ↑ Hana Ichimonme (花いちもんめ) is a traditional Japanese children’s game where two teams face each other and kinda “claim” members from the opposite side, usually while singing a playful song
  4. ↑ During Ephemeral Festival, it is said that HiMERU and Kohaku, who have come to be known as the PBB (Pastel Bee Brothers), are getting a lot of attention and working as a duo a lot.
